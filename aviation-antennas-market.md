<p><p>The aviation antennas market consists of two main types: directional antennas and omnidirectional antennas. Directional antennas are designed to focus their signal in a specific direction, providing longer range and higher gain. They are commonly used for applications such as air-to-ground communication and long-range radar systems. On the other hand, omnidirectional antennas radiate their signal in all directions, offering 360-degree coverage. They are typically used for shorter range applications, such as onboard aircraft communication systems and navigation aids. Both types play crucial roles in aviation communication and navigation.</p></p>
