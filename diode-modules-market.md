<p><p>Diode modules are electronic devices that contain different types of diodes within a single package. Fast recovery diode modules are designed to quickly recover from the forward-biasing state to the blocking state. Schottky diode modules use a metal-semiconductor junction to provide low forward voltage drop and high switching speeds. Rectifier diode modules are used to convert alternating current into direct current. Other types of diode modules may include high voltage diodes, avalanche diodes, or Zener diodes, each serving specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
